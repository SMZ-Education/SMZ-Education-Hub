What do we do?

Our main goal is to provide web presence and technology to all schools for free. We do this by following the three segments on the right. At SMZ Education, we create, maintain, and update a website, emails, and Google Maps data for your school!

Learn More
Website

SMZ Education provides your school with a free website that goes by the domain "yourschool".smzedu.com. This domain gives your school a professional website with all the features you need.

E-Mail

SMZ Education makes email addresses available to your school that go along: "yourschool"@smzedu.com. We modify as many emails as your school needs for you and your colleagues.

Help

SMZ Education hosts all the websites on our side, meaning you don't have any hassle! If you want to modify parts of your website, create new emails, etc., you can contact us at support@smzedu.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do we need to pay anything?

No, our service is 100% free! Our goal is to provide these services for free to help everybody. We do not require payment of any kind, but donations are always welcome!

What are the requirements?

There are no set requirements except that you must be an educational institution of any kind (private, state, etc.). Something to keep in mind however, is to get in touch with us to apply at support@smzedu.com.

How do I un-enroll?

Un-enrolling is a simple process, and it is sad to see you go. All you have to do is write an email to support@smzedu.com and describe your problem.

Why is your service free?

Here at SMZ Education, we are a non-profit organization that runs on donations. We want to provide websites and technology to everyone!

Which countires are elligible?

All countries around the world are accepted to SMZ Education's programmes, this map shows our current project locations!

How can I share SMZ Education?

Sharing our media is a great way to help us, consider sharing our website url, instagram account, or even LinkedIN!
