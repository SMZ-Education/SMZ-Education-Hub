Partnership With Shiloh Académie

We are very proud to announce our first ever partnership with Shiloh Académie from Gandigal-Est, Senegal. As a school whose goal it was to bring the "American Dream" to Senegal in order to prevent young Senegalese from meeting unfortunate ends while trying to immigrate to Europe via boats, we are glad to help them in their mission.
